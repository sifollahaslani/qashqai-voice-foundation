# Founding Statement

QashqAI Voice is an original cultural-technology initiative founded by **Siefollah Aslani** in 2026.

The project was established in response to the growing digital invisibility of endangered and underrepresented languages, and to the urgent need for ethical, community-centered AI pathways that protect linguistic dignity, cultural memory, and human diversity.

QashqAI Voice begins with the Qashqai language as a living cultural foundation, while developing a broader framework that can support endangered languages and marginalized linguistic communities worldwide.

The initiative stands at the intersection of:

- endangered language preservation
- cultural heritage protection
- digital inclusion
- ethical and responsible artificial intelligence
- community-centered cultural participation

QashqAI Voice was not created as a purely technical system.  
It was founded as a cultural, ethical, and strategic response to the risk of linguistic erasure in the digital age.

The initiative is guided by the principle that languages are not disposable data resources.  
They are living carriers of memory, worldview, belonging, and intergenerational knowledge.

The founding purpose of QashqAI Voice is to help build a future in which technological progress does not come at the cost of cultural disappearance.

This initiative, its mission, and its conceptual foundation originate with **Siefollah Aslani**.
