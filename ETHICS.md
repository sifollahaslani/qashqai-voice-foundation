# Ethics & Responsible AI

QashqAI Voice is committed to ethical, transparent, culturally respectful, and human-centered AI practices.

This initiative recognizes that endangered languages are not merely datasets. They are living carriers of memory, identity, dignity, worldview, and intergenerational knowledge.

The ethical foundation of QashqAI Voice is built on the protection of linguistic communities, the responsible use of technology, and the rejection of extractive or exploitative approaches to cultural data.

---

## Core Ethical Commitments

QashqAI Voice is guided by the following commitments:

- respect for cultural ownership and community rights
- informed consent in all meaningful data collection
- no exploitative use of linguistic or cultural material
- transparent use of AI-assisted processes
- human oversight in all significant decisions
- accessibility and inclusion for marginalized communities
- protection of endangered and underrepresented languages as part of global cultural justice

---

## Cultural Dignity

Languages are not raw material for technical extraction.

Each language represents a cultural world, a historical memory, and a social reality.  
QashqAI Voice treats language work as a matter of dignity, not merely innovation.

The project rejects any approach that reduces vulnerable communities to data suppliers without reciprocity, consent, recognition, or benefit.

---

## Consent and Community Participation

All voice samples, oral histories, linguistic materials, or cultural contributions should be collected, documented, and used only with informed and meaningful consent.

Whenever possible, language communities should participate not only as contributors, but as legitimate stakeholders in how their cultural and linguistic materials are represented, stored, interpreted, and used.

Community participation is not symbolic. It is part of the ethical structure of the project.

---

## Non-Extractive Data Practice

QashqAI Voice rejects extractive AI models that absorb cultural or linguistic resources without accountability.

This means:

- no hidden reuse of contributed materials
- no unapproved secondary use of voice or language data
- no commercial exploitation without explicit authorization
- no removal of cultural context from linguistic materials
- no technological development that erases community authorship or dignity

The project aims to develop a model of ethical AI that protects people as much as it advances tools.

---

## Transparency in AI Use

Where AI tools are used in research, drafting, analysis, transcription, translation, or content generation, their use should be transparent and subject to human review.

AI-assisted outputs must not be presented in misleading ways.  
Meaningful oversight remains necessary, especially in matters involving cultural representation, historical interpretation, ethical judgment, and public communication.

---

## Accessibility and Inclusion

QashqAI Voice supports digital inclusion across barriers of language, geography, class, disability, and technological access.

The project seeks to create pathways for communities that are often excluded from mainstream digital infrastructures and AI systems.

Accessibility is treated as a core ethical principle, not as an optional design feature.

---

## Governance and Human Oversight

Human judgment remains central to the governance of QashqAI Voice.

No important cultural, ethical, or strategic decision should be delegated entirely to automated systems.

AI may assist, but it does not replace responsibility.

The project supports accountable governance, reviewable processes, and the protection of community trust.

---

## Protection of Endangered Languages

QashqAI Voice understands endangered languages as part of humanity’s shared cultural inheritance.

Their preservation is not only a technical challenge, but also an ethical and civilizational responsibility.

The disappearance of a language is not simply the loss of vocabulary.  
It is the loss of memory, relationship, worldview, and human diversity.

## Ethical Position

QashqAI Voice stands for:

- ethical AI under human oversight
- community-centered language preservation
- cultural dignity in the digital age
- multilingual inclusion
- responsible innovation aligned with human rights and cultural justice
