# QashqAI Voice Manifesto

QashqAI Voice begins from a simple conviction:

**No language should disappear from the future because it was excluded from the digital present.**

We believe that endangered languages are not marginal to humanity.  
They are part of humanity’s memory, cultural intelligence, and moral inheritance.

When a language is erased, the loss is not only linguistic.  
It is historical, emotional, philosophical, ecological, and civilizational.

---

## We Reject Digital Erasure

We reject a technological future in which only dominant languages are visible, searchable, trainable, and economically valued.

A world where only high-resource languages survive in digital systems is not neutral progress.  
It is structured disappearance.

QashqAI Voice stands against that disappearance.

---

## We Reject Extractive AI

We reject models of artificial intelligence that treat communities as raw data sources while giving little back in dignity, control, visibility, or long-term benefit.

Languages are not free mines for extraction.  
Voices are not disposable inputs.  
Cultural memory is not an open resource for exploitation.

Any technological future that consumes vulnerable cultures without accountability is ethically incomplete.

---

## We Affirm Cultural Dignity

We affirm that every language carries:

- memory
- worldview
- belonging
- identity
- oral history
- intergenerational knowledge

To preserve a language is not to freeze it.  
It is to help it remain alive, visible, adaptable, and respected.

---

## We Believe in Ethical AI

We believe artificial intelligence can serve cultural preservation — but only under clear ethical conditions:

- human oversight must remain central
- consent must come before collection
- transparency must accompany AI-assisted work
- communities must be treated as partners
- innovation must not outrun dignity

AI should expand human possibility, not accelerate cultural disappearance.

---

## We Begin with Qashqai, But Not Only Qashqai

QashqAI Voice starts with the Qashqai language as a living foundation.  
But its long-term meaning is broader.

This initiative aims to contribute to a future in which endangered and underrepresented languages across the world can enter digital systems without losing their cultural integrity.

The local and the global are not opposites here.  
They are part of the same ethical pathway.

---

## We Stand for Inclusion

We stand for a digital future in which people are not excluded because of:

- language
- geography
- class
- disability
- technological access
- historical invisibility

Inclusion is not an optional feature.  
It is a condition of justice.

---

## We Build with Responsibility

QashqAI Voice is not built as a branding exercise or as a trend-driven AI experiment.

It is built as a cultural, ethical, and strategic intervention.

We aim to help create systems, archives, tools, and narratives that protect linguistic diversity and strengthen the presence of endangered languages in the age of AI.

---

## Our Position

We stand for:

- endangered language preservation
- ethical and accountable AI
- community-centered participation
- cultural rights in digital systems
- non-extractive innovation
- multilingual dignity
- human judgment over blind automation

---

## Final Commitment

QashqAI Voice exists because cultural disappearance is not inevitable.

A different future can be built — one where technology does not narrow humanity, but protects its plurality.

That future must be built consciously, ethically, and together.
